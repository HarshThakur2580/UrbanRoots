Soil Testing & Natural Amendments

pH 7.5-8.5 (Alkaline): Add sulphur or organic compost.
Low Nitrogen: Add neem cake, blood meal, or legume cover crops.
Low Potassium: Add wood ash, banana peel compost, or kelp.

Watering Wisdom

The Art of Hydrating Your Garden

When to Water?

🌅 Morning (Best): Water between 6-9 AM. Less evaporation, prevents fungal diseases.

🌙 Evening (Okay): After sunset. Leaves stay wet overnight — risk of mildew.

☀️ Midday (Avoid): Water droplets act like magnifying glass, burning leaves.

The Finger Test

Insert your finger 2 inches into soil. If dry → water deeply. If moist → wait.

💧 Pro Tip: Deep Watering

Water thoroughly until it drains from bottom. Encourages deep roots. Shallow watering = weak plants.

Desi Fertilizers

Kitchen Waste to Garden Gold

Vermicompost

Earthworm castings = Black gold. Rich in microbes, improves soil structure. Apply 1 cup per pot monthly.

Banana Peel Fertilizer

Soak peels in water for 48 hours. Use water for potassium boost. Great for flowering plants and tomatoes.

Neem Cake Powder

Natural nitrogen source + pest repellent. Mix 50g per pot before planting for healthy roots.

DIY Compost: Layer kitchen scraps (veg peels, eggshells) with dry leaves in a bin. Turn weekly. Ready in 2 months!

Harvesting & Storage Secrets

Pick leafy greens in morning for crispness. Harvest tomatoes when fully coloured. Store root veggies in cool, dark place wrapped in newspaper. Never wash before storing — moisture causes rot. For herbs like Mint & Coriander, store stems in water jar inside fridge.

📅 Best harvest time: Early morning 🔪 Use sharp, clean shears ❄️ Refrigerate within 2 hours
